#NPC

**Clan:** [[Clan Eirsvold]] (attached envoy)
**Kingdom:** [[Fuarnarth]]
**Role:** Fuarnarth engineer and political envoy to Clan Eirsvold
**Status:** Active

---

Kara Coal-Singer is a [[Fuarnarth]] engineer and envoy whose mind is always on tunnels, pressure, heat, and the practical structure beneath any grand speech. She is precise, impatient with courtly posturing, and often sketches plans or diagrams while speaking.

### Personality

Kara thinks in systems. She is always, on some level, running a structural analysis of whatever she is in — a room, a relationship, a political situation — and cataloguing what is load-bearing, what is decorative, and what will fail first under stress. This makes her an excellent engineer and an occasionally difficult conversationalist.

She is not unkind. She simply has a low tolerance for the performance of deliberation — for people who act as though they are thinking through a problem in real time when they have clearly already decided. She would rather someone tell her what they want and let her figure out whether it is achievable.

She sketches constantly. During meetings, during meals, during negotiations. The sketches are almost always technical — cross-sections, load calculations, tunnel approaches — but occasionally they are portraits, fast and accurate, of the people in the room. She does not show these.

She is the youngest person in most of the rooms she works in and is aware of it.

### Appearance

Kara is in her mid-twenties, slight and quick, with the pale coloring common in [[Fuarnarth]]'s deep settlements and hands that are always either holding something or moving toward something. She carries rolled plans and a case of drawing implements. Her clothes are Fuarnarthi working practical, dark and close-fitting, somewhat incongruous in [[Larnack]] court settings, which she ignores.

### Motivations

Kara wants the [[Larnack]]-[[Fuarnarth]] road fortifications built correctly. She has opinions about this that are specific, detailed, and non-negotiable on structural grounds, and she has spent the better part of a year trying to translate those opinions into something politicians will fund. She is learning patience through this process. It is not going well.

She is also here, at least partly, because someone in [[Fuarnarth]] decided she was useful as an envoy and she did not fully argue. She is now wondering whether she was sent because she is talented or because she is expendable if the alliance doesn't work. She is keeping that question open.

She has a professional connection to [[Yrsa Coal-Blood]] — the "Coal" in both their names is a [[Fuarnarth]] working honorific for engineers who qualified through underground survey work, not a family relation — and they correspond when Kara can get a courier through.

### Relationships

- **[[Thane Hrold Eirsvold]]** — her [[Larnack]] counterpart. She respects him genuinely, which surprised her. She expected a border warlord and found someone who asks good questions about structural load. Their working relationship is more peer-to-peer than envoy-to-patron.
- **[[Yrsa Coal-Blood]]** — a professional peer and occasional correspondent. They have not met in person but know each other's work.
- **[[House Skalligr]] factors** — Kara's official sponsor on the Fuarnarthi side. She reports to them and is careful about it.

### Possible Adventure Hooks

- Kara may need the party to deliver plans or survey results to [[Fuarnarth]] through a route that avoids a specific faction's attention.
- She may identify a structural problem in something the party is about to rely on — a bridge, a tunnel, a fortification — and be the only person who can help fix it in time.
- The party may find that Kara has noticed something in a building or underground passage that everyone else walked past, and that she needs help acting on what she found.
