#NPC

**House:** [[House Caerwyn]]
**Kingdom:** [[Larnack]]
**Role:** Lord of House Caerwyn
**Status:** Active

---

Lord Aedan Caerwyn is charming, eloquent, and deeply comfortable turning profit into policy. He prefers silk to armor, flatteries to threats, and excuses nearly every cruelty as a regrettable necessity of commerce.

### Personality

Aedan has perfected a kind of warm, expansive generosity that makes it easy to forget he is counting everything. He gives gifts, throws excellent tables, and speaks with the easy warmth of a man who has never had to want for much. He is genuinely entertaining company. He is also genuinely willing to ruin someone's livelihood if the numbers require it, and he will find a way to describe it that sounds almost kind.

He has a tendency to speak in terms of "the realm," "the broader good," and "what the markets require" — abstractions that conveniently align with whatever benefits House Caerwyn. He is aware this is what he does and does not especially care, because everyone does it; he is merely more fluent.

### Appearance

Aedan is in his early fifties, well-fed and well-dressed, with a face that has aged gracefully and knows it. He wears house colors — river-greens and pale gold — and changes his rings with his outfit. His hands are soft. He rides well but rarely far.

### Motivations

Aedan wants House Caerwyn to remain the indispensable link between [[Thiadery]]'s production and [[Larnack]]'s consumption. He is suspicious of any arrangement that cuts his house out of the middle, whether that is a direct Thiadery-Nerath trade deal or a northern route through [[House Brannoc]] territory. He prefers that people need him specifically, not merely the goods he moves.

He also has political ambitions beyond economics: he wants a Caerwyn voice at any table where [[Larnack]]'s future is decided, which means keeping rivals fractured and kings uncertain enough to need brokering.

### Relationships

- **[[Ser Gwenneth Caerwyn]]** — his daughter. He is proud of her competence and has occasionally been surprised by the limits she places on what she is willing to do. He is not entirely sure what to make of her conscience.
- **[[House Brennath]]** — a productive relationship, especially through [[Edern Brennath]]. Aedan does not fully trust Edern, which is why he respects him.
- **[[Lord Cadan Brannoc]]** — a genuine rival. Aedan finds Cadan's Nerathi nostalgia both irritating and useful to have on record.

### Possible Adventure Hooks

- Aedan may offer the party a lucrative contract that turns out to require decisions [[Ser Gwenneth Caerwyn|Gwen]] would not have authorized.
- He may need a rival merchant discredited, a route reopened, or a document that complicates a contract to disappear.
- The party may encounter people who were harmed by a Caerwyn business decision and are looking for redress Aedan cannot afford to give.
