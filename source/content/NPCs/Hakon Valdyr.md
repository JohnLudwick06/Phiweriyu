#NPC

**House:** [[House Valdyr]]
**Kingdom:** [[Fuarnarth]]
**Role:** Port operations and foreign correspondence for House Valdyr
**Status:** Active

---

Hakon Valdyr oversees much of the house's port business and foreign correspondence. He is personable by Fuarnarthi standards, which makes him particularly dangerous in negotiations.

### Personality

Hakon is the face [[Lady Astrid Valdyr]] deploys in rooms where warmth matters. He is genuinely charming in a way that functions precisely because it is not entirely performed — he actually likes people, is actually interested in their situations, and actually enjoys the social texture of port life and foreign meetings. He has just also learned to channel all of this toward his house's interests.

He has a gift for knowing when to let a negotiation breathe — when to tell a good story, share a drink, let the other party feel like they've won something — and when to quietly close a deal that was decided three meetings ago. He understands that people make decisions with their feelings and justify them with their reasoning, and he works accordingly.

He is somewhat dependent on [[Lady Astrid Valdyr|Astrid's]] oversight to prevent him from over-committing on relationships he's become genuinely attached to. He is aware of this and sometimes grateful for it.

### Appearance

Hakon is in his early thirties, well-built and deliberately well-presented in a way that reads as natural rather than effortful — this is the result of significant effort. He speaks several languages and deploys them with the ease of someone who learned them young and in context rather than from study. He smiles with his eyes, which is a skill.

### Motivations

Hakon wants the port network to grow — more counterparts, more relationships, more routes, more goods — and he wants to be the person at the center of that network. He is building a foreign correspondence network that is technically a house asset but is run almost entirely on his personal reputation, which gives him independence he does not advertise.

He is also, very privately, curious about what lies beyond the trade kingdoms he currently works with. He is not dissatisfied exactly, but he is not finished.

### Relationships

- **[[Lady Astrid Valdyr]]** — his mother. He respects her absolutely and sometimes finds her exhausting. She knows this and considers it accurate.
- **Foreign merchants, agents, and couriers** — his true professional world. He knows people in every port city adjacent to [[Fuarnarth]] and maintains those relationships through letters that are warm and commercially precise in equal measure.
- **[[Edern Brennath]]** — a counterpart Hakon finds genuinely interesting. They have overlapping information networks and have had several conversations that could have been adversarial and were not, which both of them noticed.
- **[[Captain Siofra Rhuvann]]** — the Nerathi patrol captain who has boarded his ships twice and found nothing, which Hakon considers a professional compliment to his logistics team and a reason to be careful in perpetuity. He has met her once in person, at a harbor function in [[Nerath]], and found her exactly as difficult to read as her reputation suggested. He has since made a point of keeping his Nerathi paperwork immaculate. He has also made a point of remembering everything she said that evening.

### Possible Adventure Hooks

- Hakon may hire the party to carry a package, message, or person through a route he can't use officially.
- He may be the only person who knows a specific foreign merchant, agent, or contact the party needs to find.
- The party may catch him in a commitment he made outside his mother's authorization, and have to decide what to do with that.
