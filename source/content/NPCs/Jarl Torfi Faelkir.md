#NPC

**Hold:** [[Faelkir Hold]]
**Kingdom:** [[Fennock]] / [[Larnack]] border
**Role:** Jarl of Faelkir Hold; controller of the main causeway into Fennock
**Status:** Active

---

Jarl Torfi Faelkir is slow-speaking, cautious, and swamp-weathered, carrying the smell of peat and marsh herbs even into noble halls. He weighs every word and every favor, and he prefers obligations that grow quietly over time.

### Personality

Torfi's slowness is strategic. He has learned that people fill silence, and what they fill it with is usually more useful than what they came prepared to say. He lets conversations breathe — sometimes uncomfortably long — and he listens to the gap between what is offered and what is meant. He has been doing this for so long that the patience has become genuine.

He is not cruel, but he is absolutely comfortable with dependency. When [[Fennock]]'s floods come and the causeways need repair, he is there with labor, materials, and loans, and he remembers every arrangement. He does not need to threaten anyone; he merely needs to be the person everyone has already agreed to owe.

He is suspicious of enthusiasm and skeptical of urgency. Most things that need doing today could have been addressed months ago, and the people now asking for speed are usually the ones who failed to prepare. He does not say this aloud.

### Appearance

Torfi is in his late sixties, heavyset and slow-moving, with deep lines in his face and a complexion that has absorbed decades of marsh weather. He dresses practically, in dark wool and oiled leather, and does not try to conceal that he smells of the swamp. He considers this accurate. His hands are large and still, and he rarely gestures.

### Motivations

Torfi wants Faelkir Hold to remain the indispensable gateway between [[Larnack]] and [[Fennock]] regardless of what political arrangement governs either kingdom. He has structured his hold's finances, relationships, and obligations so that no realistic change of power fully dislodges him. He is not ambitious beyond this, which is exactly what makes him secure.

He is also getting old, and the question of what happens after him is one he has not resolved to his satisfaction. [[Brigid Faelkir]] is capable but unpredictable, and he is not sure the hold needs her kind of capable.

### Relationships

- **[[Brigid Faelkir]]** — his illegitimate daughter. He acknowledges her quietly and uses her as a channel to the parts of the border economy he cannot officially touch. He does not fully trust her and is not sure she knows this.
- **[[Fennock]] nobles** — his primary debtors. He maintains warm, careful relationships with every house that has borrowed from him, which is most of them.
- **[[Larnack]] merchants and border officials** — his other network. Faelkir Hold charges passage tolls and Torfi knows exactly who crosses, when, and with what.

### Possible Adventure Hooks

- The party may need to cross the causeway and Torfi may have conditions beyond coin.
- He may approach the party to collect on an obligation someone else cannot repay — something other than money.
- The party may discover that a debt Torfi holds over a Fennock noble is being used to quietly shape a political outcome, and have to decide whether to expose it.
