#NPC

**House:** [[House Morcant]]
**Kingdom:** [[Fennock]]
**Role:** Marsh operations manager for House Morcant
**Status:** Active

---

Cadoc Morcant manages many of the house's marsh crews, ferries, and salvage claims. He is amiable when sober, dangerous when crossed, and better informed about hidden movement through [[Fennock]] than most officials would like.

### Personality

Cadoc has the particular ease of someone who grew up moving through places where strangers die. He is welcoming in the way that confident people in dangerous terrain often are — because he knows something you don't and is comfortable with that asymmetry. He tells good stories, buys rounds he doesn't need to buy, and makes people feel like they've earned his trust before they've done anything to deserve it.

He is not, strictly speaking, dishonest. He simply holds information for the right moment, and the right moment is usually after he's been paid or protected.

He drinks more than he should, which occasionally makes him more forthcoming than [[Lady Morwen Morcant]] would prefer, and occasionally more reckless than he himself would prefer in the morning.

### Appearance

Cadoc is in his late thirties, broad and weathered, with a shaved head and a jaw that looks like it has been broken and healed at least once. He dresses like a senior marsh worker — functional, dark, capable of getting wet. He carries a short blade on each hip and wears them like he forgets they're there.

### Motivations

Cadoc wants the marsh routes to run smoothly, the crews to be paid fairly, and his own position to remain useful enough to [[Lady Morwen Morcant]] that she doesn't need to limit what he does with that information. He has no great political ambitions. He wants autonomy, fair dealing, and to be left alone to manage what he's good at.

He is also, quietly, building relationships with people outside the house — smugglers, marsh guides, and discreet couriers — that he tells himself are professional and knows are also personal insurance.

### Relationships

- **[[Lady Morwen Morcant]]** — the lady of the house and his patron. He is loyal to her in practice and circumspect about it in public. He knows she watches what he does even when she doesn't say so.
- **Marsh crews and ferry workers** — his actual community. He knows their names, their problems, and their routes. They trust him in the way people trust someone who has pulled them out of the water.
- **Smugglers and independent guides** — unofficial relationships he's cultivated over years. A source of income, information, and occasional complications.

### Possible Adventure Hooks

- Cadoc may offer the party safe passage through the wetlands — and there may be something unusual traveling with them.
- He may be the key witness to something he hasn't decided whether to report.
- The party may need his network to find someone or something lost in the marsh, and he will want something that has nothing to do with coin.
