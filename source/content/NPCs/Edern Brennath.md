#NPC

**House:** [[House Brennath]]
**Kingdom:** [[Thiadery]]
**Role:** Downstream operations and trade liaison for House Brennath
**Status:** Active

---

Edern Brennath oversees the downstream movement of timber and keeps the house's relationships with barge guilds and caravan factors. He is more polished than his sister but no less calculating, and he understands exactly how much politics can be hidden inside a shipping schedule.

### Personality

Edern is sociable and easy to underestimate. He laughs readily, fills silences well, and makes a point of knowing enough about each person's situation to say something that sounds personal. Most people find him likable. Most of them are wrong to stop there.

He does not share [[Lady Rhiannon Brennath|Rhiannon's]] preference for bluntness. He prefers ambiguity because it gives him room to revise. He will commit to a price, a route, and a delivery date before he will commit to a political position, and even then only in writing he controls.

### Appearance

Edern is in his mid-thirties, taller than his sister, with a more carefully cultivated appearance. He dresses well without extravagance — the clothes of a prosperous merchant rather than a court noble — and maintains that register deliberately. He smiles often and sincerely enough that it is genuinely hard to tell when the smile means nothing at all.

### Motivations

Edern wants House Brennath's downstream relationships to remain his personal domain rather than a shared house resource. He has built those connections over years, and they give him leverage both outside the house and within it. [[Lady Rhiannon Brennath|Rhiannon]] handles the forests; he handles what happens to the wood once it moves. As long as that division holds, he has independent political standing.

He is also quietly exploring whether the house's position could be leveraged to provide information brokerage — knowing which shipments are running late, which buyers are desperate, and which routes are clear is valuable to people beyond the timber trade.

### Relationships

- **[[Lady Rhiannon Brennath]]** — his older sister. They work well together largely because they have agreed, without discussing it, not to compete for the same territory. He respects her; she trusts him with what she does not want to manage.
- **Barge guild contacts** — his primary network. He knows their debts, their rivalries, and their routes better than they might prefer.
- **[[House Caerwyn]]** — an occasional partner in downstream logistics when their interests align, which is more often than either house advertises.

### Possible Adventure Hooks

- Edern may offer the party work as discreet cargo guards — without disclosing everything the cargo contains or who else is watching it.
- He may need information recovered about a rival's shipping arrangements.
- The party may catch him facilitating a transaction he would prefer not be examined closely.
