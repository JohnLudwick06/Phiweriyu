#NPC

**House:** [[House Rhuvann]]
**Kingdom:** [[Nerath]]
**Role:** Lord of House Rhuvann
**Status:** Active

---

Lord Taran Rhuvann is a weathered sea-lord who speaks plainly by court standards and distrusts anyone who has never stood a watch in winter surf. He prefers practical measures over ceremony and often finds himself at odds with more scholarly nobles, though not with their ambitions.

### Personality

Taran is direct in the way of someone who has learned that hesitation in water costs lives. He says what he means, expects the same, and is genuinely confused by the amount of energy Nerathi court life spends on implication. He is not simple — he is simply operating with a different set of priorities that were correct in the places that shaped him.

He can be warm, particularly around sailors and those who have done hard physical work. He is terrible at small talk but excellent at the specific intimacy of shared hardship. He tells stories about bad weather, poor charts, and the decisions that kept ships alive, and those stories are almost always also about something else.

He has a temper that he has largely learned to manage, but it surfaces when he thinks someone is being deliberately oblique about a matter of safety. He once told a senior court official that he was lying in terms that were unambiguous enough to require a formal apology the next morning.

### Appearance

Taran is in his late fifties, heavyset, with a short white-grey beard and salt-bleached skin. His hands are enormous and calloused. He stands like he is on a deck — slightly wide-legged, always balanced. His court dress is correct in every formal detail and clearly uncomfortable in every informal one.

### Motivations

Taran wants [[Nerath]] to invest seriously in its maritime capacity and to treat control of coastal waters as the foundation of actual power rather than a secondary concern for trade houses. He sees the island dispute with [[Thiadery]] as an opportunity to demonstrate this, but he is not reckless — he wants the navy strong enough that the demonstration does not require a costly engagement.

He is also getting older, and he wants [[Captain Siofra Rhuvann]] positioned to inherit real operational authority before anything happens to him.

### Relationships

- **[[Captain Siofra Rhuvann]]** — his daughter. The person in the world he trusts most. He tries not to let this be obvious, with mixed success.
- **[[Lady Eithne Aedraig]]** — a working respect. They agree on the island question for different reasons and both know it.
- **Naval officers and harbor-masters** — his real constituency. He is more popular on docks than in court halls.

### Possible Adventure Hooks

- Taran may need the party to transport something — or someone — by sea under conditions he can't staff with his own people.
- He may be the only person in Nerath willing to give the party a straight answer about what is happening in coastal waters.
- A disputed ship seizure or smuggling route may require investigation he can't assign officially.
