#NPC

**House:** [[House Tiernan]]
**Kingdom:** [[Thiadery]]
**Role:** Eldest daughter of Lord Muiris Tiernan; political strategist
**Status:** Active

---

Ailis Tiernan is [[Lord Muiris Tiernan|Lord Muiris]]'s eldest daughter and the sharper political mind of the family, with a talent for turning local grievances into broader alliances. She is especially alert to the ways in which [[Larnack]] and [[Fuarnarth]] profit when Thiadery's own lords fail to act in concert.

### Personality

Ailis has her father's patience and considerably more warmth. She is genuinely interested in people — not in the manipulative sense, though she is also capable of that, but in the way that makes a person actually useful at coalition work. She remembers what people care about, follows up on it, and creates relationships that last past the immediate negotiation.

She has a gift for framing. She can take a local lord's specific, self-interested grievance and restate it as a shared Thiadery interest in a way that the lord recognizes as accurate without feeling managed. This is harder than it sounds and requires both genuine understanding and precise language. She is very good at it.

She is sometimes frustrated by the pace at which her father moves and more frustrated that she cannot say so without undermining the dynamic that makes them effective together. She has learned to be patient about his patience.

### Appearance

Ailis is in her late twenties, with her father's stillness but warmer features and the habit of smiling in a way that reaches her eyes — unusual for a Tiernan. She dresses in colors slightly brighter than her father's conservatism, which is deliberate and functions as a soft contrast that makes her more approachable at mixed gatherings. She keeps notes and usually has something to write on.

### Motivations

Ailis wants [[Thiadery]] to act as a coordinated political entity rather than a collection of competing interests that external powers exploit. She is building toward this slowly: individual relationships, specific agreements, small demonstrations that coordination actually works. She does not expect to see the full result in her father's lifetime, and is making peace with that.

She is also navigating the specific difficulty of being effective in a domain where women with power are watched differently than men with power, and she has developed a set of habits around this that she is sometimes not sure she likes.

### Relationships

- **[[Lord Muiris Tiernan]]** — her father. She knows he trusts her more than he says and is waiting for her to be ready for something specific. She does not know what it is, which is both motivating and occasionally maddening.
- **[[Lady Rhiannon Brennath]]** — a productive peer relationship. Ailis respects Rhiannon's directness and finds the timber-grain alliance a natural fit. They have had two real conversations, which is more than either of them has with most nobles.
- **[[Lady Maera Brannoc]]** — Ailis has noticed Maera across several gatherings and has formed a working theory about what she is doing that she has not yet shared with anyone.
- **[[Eluned Dunlaith]]** — a correspondence that began over timber contracts and has quietly become something more interesting. [[Fennock]]'s structural crisis makes it [[Thiadery]]'s most dependent customer, which means it is also Thiadery's most reliable point of leverage if that relationship is ever properly organized. Ailis sees Eluned as one of the few Fennocki nobles thinking past the next flood season. They have not yet met in person.

### Possible Adventure Hooks

- Ailis may approach the party as a potential channel — she needs something communicated between two lords who are not currently speaking, and cannot be seen doing it herself.
- She may need help investigating the source of a rumor that is disrupting a fragile alliance she has spent months building.
- The party may realize they are one of the few groups in the region with access to multiple factions, and Ailis may have noticed this before they did.
