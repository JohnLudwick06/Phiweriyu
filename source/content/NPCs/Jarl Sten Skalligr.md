#NPC

**House:** [[House Skalligr]]
**Kingdom:** [[Fuarnarth]]
**Role:** Jarl of House Skalligr; tunnel-lord of the deep roads
**Status:** Active

---

Jarl Sten Skalligr is a severe and disciplined tunnel-lord whose authority comes from competence more than charm. He has little patience for nobles who inherit command without understanding the structures that keep their people alive.

### Personality

Sten does not dislike people — he simply has no use for people who do not understand what they are responsible for. In [[Fuarnarth]], where the deep roads run through geologically unstable terrain and a failed pressure seal can kill a settlement, he considers ignorance of technical matters a genuine moral failure in a leader.

He speaks in short sentences and expects answers to be equally economical. He is not unkind to those who admit what they do not know; he is harsh with those who pretend. He can be generous with expertise, his own and his people's, when he believes it will be used correctly.

He has a particular respect for [[Yrsa Coal-Blood]] that he expresses mostly through giving her real work and real authority, which in Fuarnarth reads as high praise.

### Appearance

Sten is in his mid-fifties, built like someone who has spent a lifetime in tunnels — broad-shouldered, slightly hunched from low ceilings, with the careful deliberate movements of someone who learned not to bump walls with a torch. His skin is pale and his beard is cropped close for the same practical reasons Siofra keeps her hair short. He wears the insignia of his station simply, on a plain working coat.

### Motivations

Sten wants the deep roads maintained, the pressure systems operational, and the heated approaches kept open through winter. He is secondarily interested in politics, and only then when politics threatens the first three things.

He supports closer ties with [[Clan Eirsvold]] and certain [[Larnack]] factions because he believes that [[Fuarnarth]] needs reliable surface allies, not because he holds any particular affection for them. He is willing to extend hospitality to outsiders who demonstrate actual competence and complete inability to pretend he is interested in anything else.

### Relationships

- **[[Yrsa Coal-Blood]]** — his chief surveyor. He trusts her technical judgment entirely and her political judgment about seventy percent of the time, which is more than he'd grant most nobles.
- **[[House Valdyr]]** — a necessary relationship. Sten finds [[Lady Astrid Valdyr]] professionally admirable and interpersonally difficult. They do business correctly.
- **Underground engineers and maintenance crews** — Sten knows most of them by name and visits the deep passages regularly. This is not a political choice; he thinks a tunnel-lord who doesn't walk the tunnels is simply not doing the job.

### Possible Adventure Hooks

- Sten may hire the party to investigate an anomaly in a deep passage — a pressure fault, a missing survey team, or evidence of unauthorized access.
- He may grant passage through [[Fuarnarth]]'s interior in exchange for a task he cannot assign to his own people for political reasons.
- A section of deep road under his authority has been compromised in a way that looks deliberate, and he needs outside investigators.
