#NPC

**House:** [[House Skalligr]]
**Kingdom:** [[Fuarnarth]]
**Role:** Chief surveyor and political operative for House Skalligr
**Status:** Active

---

Yrsa Coal-Blood is the chief surveyor for [[House Skalligr]] and a rising political figure in her own right. She knows the furnace roads, pressure faults, and hidden warm chambers better than most priests know their liturgy.

### Personality

Yrsa is methodical, curious, and deeply engaged with the physical reality of the deep roads in a way that makes her invaluable and occasionally difficult to have a conversation with about anything else. She will notice if a tunnel has new stress fractures the same way other people notice weather. She will also tell you about them at length.

Beneath the technical preoccupation, she is politically aware in ways she does not always advertise. She has spent years moving through passages that powerful people think are secret, emerging from places where she was not expected, and overhearing things that were said assuming no one present would understand the technical context. She has learned to use that information carefully.

She is not ambitious for power as most people understand it. She is ambitious for the scope of what she gets to survey, know, and affect.

### Appearance

Yrsa is in her late thirties, wiry and sharp-featured, with coal-dust so permanently embedded in the skin creases of her hands that it no longer fully washes out — the source of her epithet, which she acquired as a working name rather than a formal title. She carries measuring instruments, chalk, and wax tablets. Her clothing is always practical and usually at least partially scorched.

### Motivations

Yrsa wants to map and understand every significant passage, chamber, and pressure system in [[Fuarnarth]] — a project that is functionally infinite, which she finds motivating rather than daunting. She also wants the knowledge she produces to actually be used, which requires political influence she is carefully building.

She is increasingly concerned about structural anomalies in sections of the deep road that she has not been able to investigate fully due to jurisdictional restrictions. She suspects something is wrong in a place she is not allowed to go, and she is working on the problem from multiple directions.

### Relationships

- **[[Jarl Sten Skalligr]]** — her patron and the person whose judgment she most trusts. She is loyal to him and works within his constraints, though she occasionally routes around them when she has to.
- **Other underground workers and minor surveyors** — Yrsa's real professional community. She trains people carefully and has a reputation among surveyors across [[Fuarnarth]] for accurate, honest work.
- **[[House Valdyr]] factors** — a complicated relationship. Yrsa has noticed that some of the passages near Valdyr port access points show signs of unusual use, and she is not sure what to do with that yet.
- - **[[Brother Malrec Aedraig]]** — a [[Nerath]] temple-administrator who provided her access to coastal survey records she needed and then did not ask for anything in return, which she has been thinking about ever since. They correspond occasionally, about nothing that would look significant to anyone reading the letters. Yrsa is aware that a man whose profession is shaping institutional memory choosing to extend a favor without naming a price is not being generous. She has not decided whether to ask him what he actually wants, because she suspects the answer will make the correspondence harder to continue, and it has been useful.

### Possible Adventure Hooks

- Yrsa may need the party to access a section of deep road that has been blocked or restricted, in exchange for guiding them safely through the interior.
- She may share information — carefully, partially — about something she found in the passages that she cannot report through official channels.
- The party may need her expertise to navigate [[Fuarnarth]]'s underground, and she may be the only person who can actually do it.
