#NPC

**House:** [[House Aedraig]]
**Kingdom:** [[Nerath]]
**Role:** Matriarch of House Aedraig
**Status:** Active

---

Lady Eithne Aedraig is the current matriarch of [[House Aedraig]], a cold and disciplined noblewoman whose courtesy never quite conceals the calculation beneath. She prefers legal language to threats and can make a simple invitation feel like a summons from history itself.

### Personality

Eithne is measured, precise, and deeply patient. She speaks rarely in public settings and chooses each word as though it will be quoted in a record. She is not cruel by nature, but she is entirely comfortable letting silence and implication do the work that crueler nobles accomplish through coercion. To those who read her well, she is dangerous precisely because she is never visibly angry.

She has a particular disdain for emotion in governance. Sentiment, to Eithne, is simply another lever to be understood and managed rather than felt. She is not without affection for her house and its legacy, but she has long since separated that feeling from any decision it might otherwise distort.

### Appearance

Eithne is silver-haired, with sharp pale eyes and a posture so exact it can seem rehearsed. She dresses in deep blues and charcoal greys, house colors that blend ceremony with austerity. She wears very little jewelry, except for a single ring bearing the Aedraig seal — she turns it with her thumb when irritated, which is the closest thing to open emotion most people ever see from her.

### Motivations

Eithne believes that Nerath's continuity depends on the maintenance of institutional memory and lawful precedent. She views House Aedraig as one of the few forces still capable of providing that continuity. She strongly opposes any concession over the disputed island taken from [[Thiadery]], not out of military aggression but because she believes capitulation would erode the authority of every ruling precedent Nerath has ever relied upon.

Her personal ambition is to see [[Brother Malrec Aedraig]] positioned where he can shape Nerath's sacred and legal institutions from within.

### Relationships

- **[[Brother Malrec Aedraig]]** — her younger brother. She supports his ambitions fully and provided the connections that placed him in his temple role. She trusts his intelligence but worries he underestimates how quickly patience runs out at court.
- **[[House Rhuvann]]** — a working relationship rather than an alliance. She respects Lord Taran's directness but believes maritime interests are too transactional to be relied on in a real crisis.
- **[[Crown of Nerath]]** — loyal in posture, though she would privately prefer a monarch more attentive to precedent and less to urgency.

### Possible Adventure Hooks

- The party may be asked to retrieve a document she holds or intercept one she is sending.
- She may hire intermediaries to locate specific archival records she cannot acquire through official channels.
- She may be a source of legal legitimacy — or the obstacle standing between the party and legal legitimacy.
