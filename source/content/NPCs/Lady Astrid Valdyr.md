#NPC

**House:** [[House Valdyr]]
**Kingdom:** [[Fuarnarth]]
**Role:** Lady of House Valdyr
**Status:** Active

---

Lady Astrid Valdyr is keen, composed, and commercially ruthless. She dislikes waste, romantic speeches, and any noble who mistakes sentiment for strategy.

### Personality

Astrid operates at a speed most nobles find uncomfortable. She has already processed the likely outcomes of a conversation before it finishes and is occasionally impatient when people work toward conclusions she reached three minutes ago. She tries to manage this impatience, with uneven success.

She is not cruel but she is transactional, and she makes no effort to disguise this. She finds the Fuarnarthi court preference for bluntness congenial, though she operates slightly warmer than pure bluntness when dealing with southern or eastern kingdoms where relationship performance matters. She considers this code-switching rather than dishonesty.

She has a genuine, if private, interest in logistics as a problem domain — in the specific pleasure of a supply chain that actually functions as designed, of contracts that are honored, of goods that arrive when they were promised. She has considered whether she would have been content as a senior factor with no house name, and concluded, honestly, probably not.

### Appearance

Astrid is in her mid-forties, sharply dressed by Fuarnarthi standards, with clothes that emphasize function and quality over ornament. Her manner of speaking is exact — she does not repeat herself and does not enjoy being asked to. Her expression at rest reads as assessment, because it usually is.

### Motivations

Astrid wants House Valdyr to be the indispensable interface between [[Fuarnarth]] and the broader trade world. She pushed the shift from [[Thiadery]] to [[Nerath]] for food purchases as a leverage play, and it largely worked. She is now working on the next move: expanding Valdyr's bonded storehouse capacity in ways that will let the house intermediate during the next regional shortage.

She is also watching the [[Thiadery]]-[[Nerath]] tension carefully. War is bad for steady trade but good for emergency contracts, and she is running both scenarios simultaneously.

### Relationships

- **[[Hakon Valdyr]]** — her son. She relies on him for the relationship management she is not built for and trusts his social intelligence, though she occasionally has to redirect him when he gets too comfortable with a partner and starts making promises she hasn't authorized.
- **[[Jarl Sten Skalligr]]** — a professionally respectful relationship. They understand each other and do not pretend otherwise. She finds his indifference to politics clarifying.
- **[[Nerath]] and [[Thiadery]] trading partners** — Astrid maintains these carefully, on both sides of the dispute, which requires careful documentation and occasional compartmentalization.

### Possible Adventure Hooks

- Astrid may need the party to verify whether a trading partner in another kingdom is honoring the terms of an agreement she cannot personally inspect.
- She may offer a commission that turns out to be part of a logistics chain she has not fully disclosed.
- The party may find that something they are investigating runs through a Valdyr storehouse, and Astrid may offer to help — for documented access to whatever they find.
