#NPC

**House:** [[House Caerwyn]]
**Kingdom:** [[Larnack]]
**Role:** Cavalry officer and route-keeper for House Caerwyn; daughter of Lord Aedan
**Status:** Active

---

Ser Gwenneth Caerwyn, often called Gwen, is the more grounded half of her house's public face. A practical cavalry officer and route-keeper, she spends more time on muddy roads and in garrison yards than at feasts, and she understands the human cost of the policies her family profits from.

### Personality

Gwen is direct without being harsh. She has her father's intelligence but not his comfort with elegant justifications — when something is costly to people, she says so plainly, which puts her at odds with [[Lord Aedan Caerwyn|Aedan's]] preferred register. She has not yet decided how far she will go in opposing his decisions when she believes them wrong, and that uncertainty troubles her more than the decisions themselves.

She is well-liked by soldiers, merchants, and guards who have actually traveled with her. She does the work alongside her people and remembers names without being prompted. She has a soldier's humor, dry and dark, that she mostly keeps to herself in formal settings.

### Appearance

Gwen is in her late twenties, lean and road-worn, with short practical hair and the faint squint of someone who has spent many hours judging distance. She prefers riding leathers and padded coats over anything ceremonial. Her hands are calloused. She keeps a short sword rather than a ceremonial blade and it shows use.

### Motivations

Gwen genuinely wants the routes and communities that House Caerwyn depends on to be stable and relatively safe — not only because it serves the house, but because she has spent enough time in those communities to think of them as something other than assets. She is increasingly uncomfortable with how much of her house's wealth depends on people having no better options.

She is considering what it would mean to push the house toward fairer terms with its tenants and trade partners — and has not yet found a way to do it that doesn't require a confrontation she is not sure she can win.

### Relationships

- **[[Lord Aedan Caerwyn]]** — her father. She respects his intelligence, inherits his acuity, and disagrees with enough of his choices that the relationship is increasingly strained under its surface cordiality.
- **[[Lady Maera Brannoc]]** — a quiet mutual respect between two women who have noticed each other's competence. Neither has made the first move toward something more.
- **Route garrisons and caravan captains** — her real community, and the people she would protect ahead of most nobles.

### Possible Adventure Hooks

- Gwen may hire or deputize the party for a route problem her father won't authorize through official channels.
- She may be the party's most reliable contact in [[Larnack]] — someone who will give them accurate information when it counts.
- She may ask for help with something that puts her at odds with her house, requiring the party to navigate that tension.
