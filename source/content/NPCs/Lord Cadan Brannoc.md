#NPC

**House:** [[House Brannoc]]
**Kingdom:** [[Larnack]]
**Role:** Lord of House Brannoc
**Status:** Active

---

Lord Cadan Brannoc is a veteran noble with an old campaign limp and a fixation on ancient laws and imperial precedent. He wears slightly outdated armor, polishes his signet ring when deep in thought, and treats politics as a continuation of old wars by quieter means.

### Personality

Cadan is blunt in a way that reads as honesty but functions as control. He uses plain speech to signal that he is not playing the courtly game — which is itself a form of the game, one he is very good at. He has a long memory for slights and a genuine respect for the kind of loyalty that predates convenience.

He is not stupid, but he is slow to update his views. Once he has decided that someone or something is trustworthy, he tends to remain loyal past the point of evidence. This makes him reliable to allies and frustratingly immovable to enemies who have already lost his regard.

### Appearance

Cadan is in his late fifties, heavyset and scarred, with a right leg that he drags slightly when tired. He keeps his beard short and his signet ring visibly prominent. He favors practical wool and leather over silk, and his armor — though outdated by two or three fashions — is meticulously maintained.

### Motivations

Cadan wants [[Larnack]] to realign meaningfully with [[Nerath]], not merely for trade, but as a kind of restoration of what he sees as the proper order: a Larnack that respects Nerathi legal tradition and positions itself as heir to the old imperial legacy. He does not want conquest. He wants legitimacy — and he believes legitimacy comes from continuity with the past.

He is also deeply protective of his house's control over the northern crossroads and will not accept any arrangement that undermines that geographic advantage, even from Nerathi allies.

### Relationships

- **[[Lady Maera Brannoc]]** — his daughter. He trusts her judgment but sometimes misunderstands what she is actually doing. He thinks she is learning. She is leading.
- **[[Crown of Nerath]]** — a sincere admirer, though he'd be disappointed to know how little they think of him.
- **[[House Caerwyn]]** — a grudging rival. Caerwyn's eastern wealth and Thiadery ties represent everything Cadan distrusts.

### Possible Adventure Hooks

- Cadan may ask the party to recover a document he claims proves an old land right or treaty obligation.
- He may need discreet escorts for a Nerathi delegation that he cannot officially sponsor.
- His certainty about an old ally's loyalty may put both him and the party in danger.
