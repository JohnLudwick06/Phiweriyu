#NPC

**House:** [[House Brannoc]]
**Kingdom:** [[Larnack]]
**Role:** Daughter of Lord Cadan Brannoc; political operative
**Status:** Active

---

Lady Maera Brannoc is sharp, watchful, and far more politically dangerous than her youth suggests. She rarely drinks, keeps herself near exits at social gatherings, and remembers names, debts, and slights with unnerving precision.

### Personality

Maera presents as attentive and deferential, particularly around her father and older nobles. This is a considered performance. She listens more than she speaks, which means she almost always knows more than whoever is speaking. When she does offer an opinion, it is usually the one that shifts the conversation.

She is not cruel and is not especially motivated by revenge or dominance. She wants outcomes. If a rival's cooperation serves her goals, she will cooperate. If someone must be discredited, she would rather it look like their own doing. She has a low tolerance for waste, including wasted enmity.

### Appearance

Maera is in her mid-twenties, composed and precise in her dress without being extravagant. She tends toward muted colors — greens and dark browns. She keeps her hair pinned tight when working, loose only when she wants to seem less formidable. She rarely wears jewelry that could snag or make noise.

### Motivations

Maera wants House Brannoc to survive the coming realignment of kingdoms regardless of which direction it falls. She shares her father's Nerathi sympathies but is not ideologically committed to them — if [[Thiadery]] offered better terms, she would advise her father to take them. She is building a network of contacts, debts, and favors that does not depend on any single faction's success.

She is also quietly aware that her father's instincts are becoming a liability, and she is slowly positioning herself to hold the house together when he is no longer capable.

### Relationships

- **[[Lord Cadan Brannoc]]** — her father. She loves him genuinely and protects his reputation even as she routes around his blind spots. She has not yet decided how much she will let him know.
- **[[House Caerwyn]]** — Maera respects [[Ser Gwenneth Caerwyn|Gwen Caerwyn]] more than she would ever admit to her father. She considers Gwen the only other player at Larnack courts who operates without self-delusion.
- **Various couriers, merchants, and minor officials** — her real network, invisible to anyone looking only at houses.

### Possible Adventure Hooks

- Maera may hire the party for a task she will describe as routine and is not.
- She may need something retrieved or handled without connecting back to House Brannoc.
- If the party gains her trust, she may be the best source of accurate intelligence at any [[Larnack]] court scene.
