House Caerwyn is rooted in the fertile eastern riverlands of Larnack and profits immensely from its ties to Thiadery. Grain, timber, and barge traffic form the core of its power. Publicly, Caerwyn speaks the language of feeding the realm and ensuring fair prices. Privately, it seeks to make cities and nobles dependent on food and materials moving through its hands.

### Lord Aedan Caerwyn

Lord Aedan Caerwyn is charming, eloquent, and deeply comfortable turning profit into policy. He prefers silk to armor, flatteries to threats, and excuses nearly every cruelty as a regrettable necessity of commerce.

### Ser Gwenneth Caerwyn

Ser Gwenneth, often called Gwen, is the more grounded half of her house’s public face. A practical cavalry officer and route-keeper, she spends more time on muddy roads and in garrison yards than at feasts, and she understands the human cost of the policies her family profits from.
