[[Mordake.png.png]]
Forged for a king whose name has long since been erased from history and now known as the [[Fallen Hero]], Mordake was once a sacred blade carried in defense of crown and kingdom. Its steel was said to answer only to unwavering conviction, and in the king’s early years it became a symbol of justice, loyalty, and divine rule.

But when the [[Old Empire]] collapsed beneath betrayal and war, the king refused to yield his throne or accept death. In the ruins of his court, he bound himself to ancient and forbidden powers so that he might endure beyond the fall of his kingdom. Mordake endured with him.

Over the years the blade changed alongside its master. What was once a holy weapon became stained by grief, wrath, and the countless lives taken in the name of a kingdom already dead. Dark energy now coils within the sword, feeding on broken vows and lingering sorrow.

Even now, long after the kingdom’s fall, Mordake remains impossibly sharp. Those who wield it can feel the echo of its final oath lingering within the steel the unending promise of a king who refused to let go.
