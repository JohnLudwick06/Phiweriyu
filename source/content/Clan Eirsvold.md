Clan Eirsvold rules mineral-rich hill country and the fortified roads that connect Larnack to Fuarnarth. The clan advocates stronger fortifications, better armories, and deeper military cooperation, all under the banner of protecting the realm. In truth, Eirsvold hopes to bind Larnack’s martial future to Fuarnarth’s strength in engineering, metallurgy, and warcraft.

### Thane Hrold Eirsvold

Thane Hrold Eirsvold is a scarred veteran who smells of smoke and forge oil and judges people as bluntly as he judges iron. He values strength, discipline, and readiness, and sees politics largely through the lens of whether a kingdom is prepared to survive the next crisis.

### Kara Coal-Singer

Kara Coal-Singer is a Fuarnarth engineer and envoy whose mind is always on tunnels, pressure, heat, and the practical structure beneath any grand speech. She is precise, impatient with courtly posturing, and often sketches plans or diagrams while speaking.
