Jarl Faelkir holds the dike-town that guards the main causeway into [[Fennock]]’s swamps from [[Larnack]]. His faction benefits from Fennock’s instability, controlling key crossings, repair work, loans, and the movement of materials into the sinking city. He wraps this power in the language of protection and stewardship, but much of it rests on dependency.

### Jarl Torfi Faelkir

Jarl Torfi Faelkir is slow-speaking, cautious, and swamp-weathered, carrying the smell of peat and marsh herbs even into noble halls. He weighs every word and every favor, and he prefers obligations that grow quietly over time.

### Brigid Faelkir

Brigid Faelkir, his illegitimate daughter, moves easily between the worlds of smugglers and courtiers. Restless, observant, and hard to pin down, she represents the younger generation most likely to reject the hypocrisies of the existing order.
