

The crown of [[Nerath]] rules a kingdom that believes history confers duty as much as privilege. Nerath’s monarchy is therefore more ceremonial in appearance than some others, but that ceremony should not be mistaken for weakness. The crown draws authority from its association with the oldest city on the mainland, from religious sanction, and from its ability to arbitrate among houses that all imagine themselves stewards of a great inheritance.

The king’s central challenge is not creating legitimacy but directing it. Every noble in Nerath understands the language of tradition, which means every dispute can be framed as a defense of the kingdom’s proper order. The crown must constantly decide which traditions to honor, which to reinterpret, and which to quietly set aside for the sake of peace.
