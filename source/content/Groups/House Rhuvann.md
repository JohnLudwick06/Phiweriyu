#House

House Rhuvann is a maritime power within [[Nerath]], enriched by shipyards, harbor dues, island traffic, and the armed escort of coastal shipping. Where houses like [[House Aedraig]] derive authority from antiquity and ritual, Rhuvann grounds its prestige in command of the sea. Its captains, pilots, and harbor-masters believe that no kingdom truly controls a coastline unless it can enforce that claim in bad weather and under hostile eyes.

Because of this, House Rhuvann has been one of the chief practical beneficiaries of the island dispute with [[Thiadery]]. Control of nearby waters justifies fortification, patrol contracts, and expanded naval spending, all of which increase the house’s importance. Publicly, Rhuvann insists that it merely protects lawful Nerathi holdings. Privately, many within the house see the crisis as proof that Nerath must lean further into maritime strength if it wishes to remain more than a museum of old grandeur.

### [[Lord Taran Rhuvann]]

Lord Taran Rhuvann is a weathered sea-lord who speaks plainly by court standards and distrusts anyone who has never stood a watch in winter surf. He prefers practical measures over ceremony and often finds himself at odds with more scholarly nobles, though not with their ambitions.

### [[Captain Siofra Rhuvann]]

Captain Siofra Rhuvann commands several of the house’s patrol vessels and has earned a reputation for being both disciplined and unforgiving. She treats smuggling, espionage, and diplomatic ambiguity as the same problem viewed from different distances.
