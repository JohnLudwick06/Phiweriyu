#House
House Brannoc controls the northern crossroads leading toward [[Nerath]] and is the strongest Nerath-leaning faction within [[Larnack]]. It presents itself as a defender of roads, law, and imperial memory, and favors closer ties with Nerath’s scholars, merchants, and traditions. Beneath that posture, Brannoc wants to strengthen Nerathi prestige inside Larnack and to place itself at the center of any future restoration of old imperial legitimacy.

### [[Lord Cadan Brannoc]]

Lord Cadan Brannoc is a veteran noble with an old campaign limp and a fixation on ancient laws and imperial precedent. He wears slightly outdated armor, polishes his signet ring when deep in thought, and treats politics as a continuation of old wars by quieter means.

### [[Lady Maera Brannoc]]

Lady Maera Brannoc is sharp, watchful, and far more politically dangerous than her youth suggests. She rarely drinks, keeps herself near exits at social gatherings, and remembers names, debts, and slights with unnerving precision.
