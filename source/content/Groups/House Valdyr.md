#House

House Valdyr is a surface-and-port power in [[Fuarnarth]], enriched by the difficult exchange between subterranean industry and the wider world beyond the cold coast. Where houses like [[House Skalligr]] rule the deep approaches, Valdyr profits from making Fuarnarth’s output legible and movable to other kingdoms. Its granaries, dockyards, bonded storehouses, and caravan arrangements give it influence wherever southern goods pass into foreign hands.

The house was among the strongest advocates for shifting food purchases from [[Thiadery]] to [[Nerath]], seeing the change not simply as a supply decision but as leverage. Valdyr understands that trade is policy by other means, and it has little sentimental attachment to old arrangements if better terms can be extracted elsewhere.

### [[Lady Astrid Valdyr]]

Lady Astrid Valdyr is keen, composed, and commercially ruthless. She dislikes waste, romantic speeches, and any noble who mistakes sentiment for strategy.

### [[Hakon Valdyr]]

Hakon Valdyr oversees much of the house’s port business and foreign correspondence. He is personable by Fuarnarthi standards, which makes him particularly dangerous in negotiations.
