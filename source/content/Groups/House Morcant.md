#House
House Morcant is a fenland noble line in [[Fennock]], controlling raised settlements, eel waters, reed-cutting grounds, and the tolls on several difficult but valuable marsh passages. The house has a reputation for surviving where cleaner and prouder families would simply drown. It excels at small margins of advantage accumulated over years: a safe crossing here, a salvage right there, a quiet debt remembered when the floods rise.

Morcant’s influence has grown as the capital’s instability has deepened. Whenever districts sink, pilings fail, or new causeways must be laid, houses with command over labor, barges, and marsh navigation become harder to ignore. Morcant insists that it serves the kingdom by keeping the wettest regions connected. In truth, the house understands that disaster turns geography into political capital.

### [[Lady Morwen Morcant]]

Lady Morwen Morcant is unsentimental, sharp-eyed, and difficult to surprise. She speaks with the calm certainty of someone who has watched grand promises disappear beneath black water more than once.

### [[Cadoc Morcant]]

Cadoc Morcant manages many of the house’s marsh crews, ferries, and salvage claims. He is amiable when sober, dangerous when crossed, and better informed about hidden movement through Fennock than most officials would like.
