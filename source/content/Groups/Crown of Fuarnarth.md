
The crown of [[Fuarnarth]] stands atop a kingdom where rulership is judged by whether the systems of survival continue to function. In warmer and softer lands, kings may be measured by pageantry or generosity. In Fuarnarth, a ruler is respected when roads stay open, tunnels stay warm, stores are adequate, and the realm is not caught unprepared by winter, collapse, or interruption.

As a result, the crown tends to command a sterner kind of loyalty than many outsiders expect. Nobles may argue fiercely over trade, engineering priorities, and foreign entanglements, but they understand that failure at the top can become catastrophe below the earth. Authority in Fuarnarth is therefore practical before it is theatrical.
