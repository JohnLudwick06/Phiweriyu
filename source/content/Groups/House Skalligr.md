#House

House Skalligr is a deep-road and furnace house of [[Fuarnarth]], with authority over several of the heated tunnel approaches that connect mining settlements to the kingdom’s more habitable chambers and surface keeps. Its prestige comes from controlling movement through dangerous underground routes and from keeping those routes maintained under brutal conditions. In Fuarnarth, this is not merely a logistical function but a form of sovereignty.

Skalligr favors strength, technical competence, and hard bargaining with outsiders. It is among the houses most supportive of closer ties with martial factions in [[Larnack]], particularly those like [[Clan Eirsvold]] that respect Fuarnarth’s systems rather than merely its metal. The house does not seek war recklessly, but it believes peace has value only when backed by the ability to survive interruption.

### [[Jarl Sten Skalligr]]

Jarl Sten Skalligr is a severe and disciplined tunnel-lord whose authority comes from competence more than charm. He has little patience for nobles who inherit command without understanding the structures that keep their people alive.

### [[Yrsa Coal-Blood]]

Yrsa Coal-Blood is his chief surveyor and a rising political figure in her own right. She knows the furnace roads, pressure faults, and hidden warm chambers better than most priests know their liturgy.
