#House

House Aedraig is one of the oldest and most ceremonial noble lines in [[Nerath]], with estates tied closely to the ancient city and to the institutions that preserve law, memory, and ritual continuity. The house presents itself as guardian of proper order, arguing that Nerath’s strength lies not merely in age but in disciplined inheritance. In practice, House Aedraig uses that posture to influence judges, priests, archivists, and scribal offices, giving it power that often appears respectable rather than overtly coercive.

The house is among the strongest advocates for holding the disputed island taken from [[Thiadery]]. To Aedraig, retreat would be more than a military concession; it would be a public admission that history can be bargained away. Its members therefore speak often of precedent, solemn duty, and the cost of appearing irresolute. They do not necessarily desire open war, but they are dangerously willing to accept escalating pressure so long as Nerath’s prestige remains intact.

### [[Lady Eithne Aedraig]]

Lady Eithne Aedraig is the current matriarch of the house, a cold and disciplined noblewoman whose courtesy never quite conceals calculation. She prefers legal language to threats and can make a simple invitation feel like a summons from history itself.

### [[Brother Malrec Aedraig]]

Brother Malrec is a younger son turned temple-administrator, deeply learned and more ambitious than his vows suggest. He serves as a bridge between noble interests and sacred institutions, quietly shaping which histories are remembered, emphasized, or allowed to fade.
