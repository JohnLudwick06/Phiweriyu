#House

House Tiernan is among the most influential agrarian powers in [[Thiadery]], holding broad estates of grain land, mill rights, and river storage along some of the kingdom’s most productive routes. It is a house defined by harvest mathematics, tenant obligations, and the politics of supply. In a realm where food is power, Tiernan rarely needs to shout in order to be heard.

The house believes that Thiadery’s comparative weakness against [[Nerath]] comes not from lack of resources but from lack of discipline among the kingdom’s own great lords. As a result, Tiernan often pushes for tighter coordination of grain levies, convoy protection, and bargaining strategy. This is not born from love of central authority so much as frustration that Thiadery’s abundance is too often converted into leverage for others.

### [[Lord Muiris Tiernan]]

Lord Muiris Tiernan is a patient and unsentimental landholder who treats shortages, tariffs, and border incidents as problems of timing and pressure. He is rarely the loudest man in a room, but he is often the one everyone must reckon with once provisions become scarce.

### [[Ailis Tiernan]]

Ailis Tiernan is his eldest daughter and the sharper political mind of the family, with a talent for turning local grievances into broader alliances. She is especially alert to the ways in which [[Larnack]] and [[Fuarnarth]] profit when Thiadery’s own lords fail to act in concert.
